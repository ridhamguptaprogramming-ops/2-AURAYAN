Our Story

Born From Heritage. Made For The World.

AURAYAN is built on the philosophy that fragrance is identity. Born from a deep passion for luxury and inspired by the royal heritage of Indian emperors, we set out to create fragrances that transcend borders and speak to the modern individual who values presence, power, and legacy.

Our journey began with a simple question: "What if a fragrance could be a crown‚Äîinvisible, yet felt by everyone?" This vision led us to blend Indian royal heritage with Middle Eastern richness and European perfumery science to create something truly global.

Every bottle of AURAYAN is carefully crafted using globally sourced luxury oils and designed for long-lasting performance in the Indian climate while maintaining international quality standards. We don't create perfumes for trends. We create signatures for personalities.
