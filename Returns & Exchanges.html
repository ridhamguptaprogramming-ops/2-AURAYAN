Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between returns and exchanges?

Returns give you a full refund within 30 days. Exchanges let you swap your product for a different AURAYAN fragrance within 14 days. Both are completely free!

Do I pay for return or exchange shipping?

No! We provide prepaid return shipping labels for both returns and exchanges. You won't pay anything for shipping.

Can I return a product and then exchange later?

No, you must choose either return or exchange at the time of request. However, once you receive your refund from a return, you can purchase a new product using that refund.

What if the product arrives damaged?

Contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll immediately send a replacement or refund, whichever you prefer, at no cost to you.

How long does the refund take?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed in 5-7 business days. It may take an additional 3-5 days for the amount to appear in your account depending on your bank.

Can I exchange for a higher-priced fragrance?

Exchanges are for products of equal or lesser value. For upgrades to higher-priced items, you'd need to pay the difference or opt for a return and new purchase.

What if I don't have my order number?

Contact us at returns@aurayan.com or exchanges@aurayan.com with the email address on your account, and we'll help you locate your order.
