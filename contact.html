Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within India takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping is available for orders over ‚Çπ2,000 with 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping is also available with estimated delivery of 7-14 business days depending on location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your fragrance, you can return it within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. The product must be unused and in original packaging.

Can I exchange my fragrance for a different one?

Yes, we offer free exchanges within 14 days of purchase. The product must be unused and in original condition. Simply contact our customer support team to initiate an exchange.

Do you offer samples or testers?

Absolutely! We offer fragrance sample sets so you can try different scents before committing to a full bottle. Sample sets are available for purchase at a discounted price.

Are your products authentic?

Yes, all AURAYAN products are 100% authentic and crafted with premium ingredients. We use only ethically sourced, high-quality components and follow international standards for fragrance production.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to 50+ countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Shipping costs vary by location and order value.

Can I purchase wholesale?

Yes, we offer wholesale and bulk purchase options for retailers and resellers. Please contact our wholesale team at wholesale@aurayan.com for pricing and availability.

How should I store my fragrance?

Store your fragrance in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Keep the bottle tightly sealed to prevent evaporation. Avoid storing in bathrooms as humidity can affect the fragrance.
